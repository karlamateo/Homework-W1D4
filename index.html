WHICH IS BETTER?

It is not only a color that is usually connected to many beautiful things in nature like the sky, gorgeous beach water or even yummy blueberries. But it is even believed to have positive psychological effects like encouraging a feeling of calm and security.

While others would argue that red is the best. It is the color seen on natures simple pleasures and beautiful landscapes. From delicious red apples to acre to endless rows of rose bushes. It's also commonly associated with life/health, love, passion and danger in modern day society.
